What We Do.

The Underground Garden Project was created as a way to unite the community in South Florida around a love for music, dance, and one another. As an event management company, we provide solutions to promoters interested in having a high quality, well-coordinated, and minimal-risk event.

Corporate Identity

Underground Garden LLC. was founded in 2021 as two event companies in the South Florida area merged wiith a similar goal in mind: to bring the community together through music, art, and dance. We as a team and as a company have dedicated ourselves to providing high-quality experiences to the community.

The love we share with our community is the fuel that drives us. None of this would be possible without the people we are fortunate enough to serve.
